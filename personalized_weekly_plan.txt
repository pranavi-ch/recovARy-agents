**1-Hour Home Exercise and Cognitive Plan**

**Warm-up (5 minutes)**

* Seated marching: 3 sets of 10 reps
* Toe tapping: 3 sets of 10 reps (each foot)
* Seated shoulder rolls: 3 sets of 10 reps

**Physical Therapy Exercise (20 minutes)**

* Seated leg lifts: 3 sets of 10 reps (lift each leg 6-8 inches off the floor)
* Seated arm raises: 3 sets of 10 reps (raise each arm 10-12 inches off the shoulder)
* Seated leg stretches: 3 sets of 30 seconds per leg (focus on quadriceps and hamstrings)
* Seated arm circles: 3 sets of 10 reps (10 inches in diameter)

**Cognitive Exercises (15 minutes)**

* Repeat the following phrases 5 times each: "Remember to take your medications," "Don't forget to eat breakfast," and "Call your doctor if you experience [specific symptom]"
* Use logic to solve the following puzzle: "If Sally has 5 dogs and gives 2 away, how many dogs does Sally have left?" (write the answer on a piece of paper)
* Complete the following pattern: 2, 5, 8, 11, _______ (write the next number in the pattern)

**Cool-down (10 minutes)**

* Seated deep breathing exercises: focus on slow, deep breaths for 5 minutes
* Progressive muscle relaxation: tense and relax each major muscle group for 5 minutes
* Stretching: perform any additional stretches as needed or recommended by physical therapist
