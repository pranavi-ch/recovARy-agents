**Home Exercise Plan**

**Objective:** To perform cognitive and physical exercises for 1 hour while at home, focusing on improving cognitive function and physical mobility.

**Exercises:**

**Physical Therapy (30 minutes)**

1. Upper Body Stretching: Gently stretch your arms, shoulders, and wrists for 2 sets of 10 repetitions.
	* Hold for 15 seconds, release for 5 seconds, and repeat.
2. Seated Leg Lifts: Lift your legs while seated, 2 sets of 10 repetitions.
	* Hold for 5 seconds, release for 5 seconds, and repeat.
3. Wall Squats: Stand with your back against the wall, 2 sets of 10 repetitions.
	* Hold for 5 seconds, release for 5 seconds, and repeat.

**Cognitive Exercises (20 minutes)**

1. Word Recall: Write down 10 words on a piece of paper, and then try to recall them from memory.
	* Repeat this exercise 2-3 times.
2. Mnemonic Exercise: Create a sentence using the first letter of each word to help you remember a list of 5 words.
	* Repeat this exercise 2-3 times.
3. Random Number Generation: Close your eyes and try to remember a sequence of 5 random numbers.
	* Repeat this exercise 2-3 times.

**Break (10 minutes)**

Take a 10-minute break to rest and recharge.

**Additional Tips:**

* Perform exercises in a quiet, distraction-free area.
* Use a timer to keep track of time.
* If you experience any discomfort or pain, stop the exercise and consult with your healthcare provider.
* Stay hydrated by drinking water throughout the exercise routine.

**Remember:** This plan is tailored to your specific needs and goals. Be sure to adjust the exercises as needed and consult with your healthcare provider if you experience any changes or concerns.
